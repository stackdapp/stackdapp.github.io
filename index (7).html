Why Stackd works

Built different,
for kids who think different.

Most apps are checklists. Stackd is a visual day — with timers, XP, and rewards that make routine feel like a game, not a chore.

🗓

Schedule, not a to-do list

Kids see their whole day laid out with time blocks — Wake Up, School, Free Time, Bedtime. No surprises, no meltdowns.

⚡

XP for everything

Every task completed, every block done earns XP. Kids level up through ranks and spend points on rewards you set.

⏱

Gentle transition warnings

The school timer shows a soft color shift and chime before time's up. Transitions aren't a surprise anymore.

🔒

Parent PIN control

Everything is set up by you, locked behind a PIN. Kids can use the app freely — they just can't edit their own rewards.

🎨

5 themes, their choice

Special Ops, Hero, Enchanted, Adventure, or Calm. The app feels like theirs — because it is.

📵

No account, no ads, offline

Save to your home screen and it works anywhere. No login, no subscription, no data collected. Just the app.

Two versions

Pick what fits your family.

Use one, or both. Each is a standalone app saved to your home screen.
